ABOUT US

SVCE is a unit of Sri Venkateswara Educational and Health Trust (SVEHT). The trust was founded on 1 August 1985 at the behest and benign blessings of His Holiness Pujashree Kanchi Kamakoti Peedathipathi Sri Jayendra Saraswathi Swamigal. Dr. A. C. Muthiah, a renowned engineer, industrialist and philanthropist, is the Chairman of the Governing Council of the college. SVCE is one of the most renowned and brightest campuses in the country. Students gain from worldclass academic infrastructure and professional-level athletic facilities in addition to the advantages listed below.

  • One of the prestigious and top ranked Autonomous engineering institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai Accredited by NAAC and NBA
  • Over 28 % of the alumni work abroad
  • Highest placement offers of Rs.25 LPA and 20 LPA in PayPal and Amazon
  • Highly qualified faculty and staff with an average experience of over 20 years
  • World class Laboratories to foster innovation and research
  • Alumni working in fortune 500 companies like Google, Microsoft, Facebook, Mercedes,Benz, INTEL, etc.,
  • State-of-the- art-campus with modern amenities in the industrial corridor of Chennai

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional Non-Profit Society for the Technical Education System in our nation functioning with the motto of Career and Personality Development of Students as well as the overall development of our Technical Education System. ISTE efficiently supports various goals of the Government as the only national association of educators in the field of engineering and technology. ISTE is having collaboration and MoUs with government organsations and various Universities for the promotion of technical education system in the country. At the national level, ISTE has an Executive Council. More than 128500 technical professors, 535,000 students, more than 2740 institutions (including IITs, IISc., NITs, and other top technical institutes), 1414 faculty chapters, 1505 student chapters, and 19 Sections make up its active membership at the national and state levels, respectively.

ISTE conducts the annual convention for students and teachers separately every year, and many technocrats, technical educators, policymakers, industry professionals, etc. attend and have discussions. For the proposed State Level Annual Engineering Students Convention-2023, the renowned experts in technical education are invited to deliver special lectures, and delegates will present research papers. ISTE actively participates in numerous initiatives carried out by the National Board of Accreditation New Delhi and the All India Council for Technical Education New Delhi (AICTE) (NBA)
